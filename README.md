# Manga Tracker

A social platform for manga enthusiasts to discover, track, rate, and review manga - inspired by Letterboxd and IMDb.

## Overview

Manga Tracker lets users catalog their manga reading experience, share reviews, create lists, and connect with other manga readers. Whether you're tracking ongoing series, discovering hidden gems, or sharing your thoughts on the latest releases, this platform brings the manga community together.

## Key Features

### Core Functionality
- **Manga Database**: Comprehensive catalog of manga titles with detailed information (author, artist, genres, publication status, volumes, chapters)
- **User Profiles**: Personalized profiles showcasing reading history, ratings, reviews, and lists
- **Rating System**: Rate manga on a scale (e.g., 1-10 or 5-star system)
- **Reviews**: Write and share detailed reviews with spoiler tags
- **Reading Status**: Track manga as "Reading", "Completed", "Plan to Read", "Dropped", or "On Hold"

### Social Features
- **Lists**: Create and share custom lists (e.g., "Best Shounen", "Underrated Gems", "Top 10 of 2024")
- **Activity Feed**: See what friends are reading, rating, and reviewing
- **Comments & Discussions**: Engage with other users' reviews and lists
- **Following System**: Follow users with similar taste

### Discovery
- **Recommendations**: Personalized suggestions based on reading history and ratings
- **Trending**: Popular and currently trending manga
- **Search & Filter**: Advanced search by genre, demographic, author, status, etc.
- **Year in Review**: Annual statistics and highlights

### Statistics
- **Reading Stats**: Track total volumes/chapters read, reading streaks, time spent
- **Taste Profile**: Analyze favorite genres, authors, and reading patterns
- **Community Stats**: See how your ratings compare to the community
